Graphic Design

Design that performs — not the design awards circuit.

I design with one constraint: the visual has to do its job. Whether it's a POSM at a clinic counter, an SEO featured image fighting for the click, or a landing page that has to convert a $20 ad click, I treat every design as a conversion asset. Tools I use daily: Adobe Illustrator, Canva Pro + AI, Premiere Pro, CapCut, and an evolving stack of AI-assisted design tools.
