Social Media

Personal brands that compound — for mainstream audiences and niche experts.

I build personal brands for two distinct audience types. For mainstream creators — doctors, lifestyle experts, public-facing professionals — I focus on engagement, watch-time, and trust signals at scale. For niche specialists — feed industry experts, academic researchers, B2B thought leaders — I focus on authority and converting attention into business outcomes. Across both, I run multi-platform content engines covering TikTok, LinkedIn, Facebook, YouTube, Reels, and Zalo.
